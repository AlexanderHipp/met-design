The city that never sleeps

In 2012 I went to New York for one week. It was great to see all the Skyscrapers and to life the special New Yorker way of life. The sun was boiling and we have done all the tourist trips.

We visited Ellis Island, the Times Square (multiple times to be honest) and had the great view from the "Top of the Rock". Our Hotel was located in China Town, quite a separate city in the middle of the Big Apple. They are right, when they say: There is no other place like New York (maybe London).

Lonely beaches and mountains

In the summertime 2012 we made a road trip with a 9-seater from Germany to Corsica. 28 hours later and after we visited Switzerland, Italy and a ferry we arrived in Corsica.

For the next two weeks it felt like the worls stands still. No Stress and so many lonely and quiet beaches. We drove around the island, hiked in the mountains and swam in the river.

Aloha Lifestyle

From October 2012 to January 2013 I was in Hawaii, better in Honolulu, for an internship. Ok it was more like surfing, hiking and just laying at the beach to be honest.

But they told me, that is the hawaiian way of life. Everything is about surfing in Hawaii or other sports you can do. There are so many possibilities. I had one of my best weekends ever there. We went to the north coast to watch the World Cup of surfing. The waves were quiet big that day.

Go Bruins, Celtics and Red Sox

In Boston is everything about there four big teams.

During the spring in 2013 I went to Boston for an internship at a game company. My appartement was real close to the beach, it was just perfect.

I did trips to Cambridge, to New Hamption and Maine. But the real Boston atmosphere was something very special.

My home

During the years 2012 to 2014, I visited some very special places around my hometown Tuttlingen. These are just a view impression what my country looks like.

Mostly from Karlsruhe, where my university is located, or from Munich and Stuttgart. The to biggest cities in Southern Germany. My favorite of all time the lake of constance is also presented.

A dream comes true

One of my favorite places I always wanted to visit is Rome. So my brother and me did a little 4-days trip to the Italian capital in spring 2014.

The ancient city is full with architectural masterpieces and there is history at every corner. Everyone should visit the "eternal city" at least once in their lifetime. Drink a real italian coffee, eat italian ice or did I mentioned the pizza and pasta. So many delicious food.

5 islands in a week

In spring 2014 my family and I did a cruise trip with AIDA to the Canary Islands. It was a great experience, because every morning you wake up it is a completely different islands out there.

So we visited Gran Canaria, Lanzarote, Fuerteventura, Madeira and Tenerife. The flora and fauna of each island is so special and different from the others. You would never think they belong together.

Diffent culture - different rules

In summer 2014 I visited my brother and friends in Shanghai for two weeks. The city is absolutely phenomenal and the fact that back in 1993 there were no Skyscrapers at all.

We went by the fast-track train from the very "western-orientated" Shanghai to Beijing. that wwas a completely different world. So much history concentrated on one spot in china. The great wall and the forbidden city are only two highlights of the trip I will never forget.

Please mind the gap

At the moment I live in London to study and doing an internship.

London is the city in the world with the most cultural variety ever and you can really feel it. Just go to Bricklane Market on a Sunday to taste something to eat from almost every country in the world. The city is so busy and vibrant. I really could image to life here more then a few months.

Cold but beautiful

The destination of one of my next trips will be definitely a Scandinavian country. I would love to visit Stockholm or Oslo, but also going to a Fjord on a boat.

A little dream is to go camping there, just fishing your lunch from a lake and relax.

Backpacking in South America

I would love to backpack around the stunning countries Bolivia, Peru, Colombia, Ecuador and Paraguay. Just take the backpack and travel for a few weeks around. Visiting Machu Pichu or going maybe to Brazil as well. We will see.

Middle-earth

My last dream destination is New Zealand. After watching the Lord of the rings and the Hobbit movies, where all the country footage was made in New Zealand. I have to go there and visit the great movie locations. The countryside is so beautiful, with lots of mountain chains, lakes and rivers everywhere.
